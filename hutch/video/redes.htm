Cuervos, Bonobos y Otros Espejos Donde Vernos

While working at the Center for Adaptive Behavior and Cognition at the Max Plank Institute for Human Development in Berlin, I was interviewed for this documentary on decision making.

The four-minute section dealing with my work is available here (download the 10 Mb video file and play with e.g. Windows Media Player; you may need to download an Xvid codec, available from here or here). The commentary is in Spanish, but, even if you can't follow that, you can watch me (!) and also see the interesting MPIB building where I worked. The weather in Berlin is really not always like this!

The Redes web site still provides a description of the programme.
