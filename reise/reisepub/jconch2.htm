Update

Three new vice-county records reported by Ben Rowson are published in: Norris, A. 2010 Non-marine recording—activity and highlights 2009. Mollusc World 23:13–14. These are from Knowle, Bristol (VC 6), Newport (VC 35), and Caerphilly (VC 41). Two of these records appear to predate our record, but this is an error: they all postdate it (B. Rowson, personal communication 7.3.11).
