H. REISE, 2007. A review of mating behavior in slugs of the genus Deroceras (Pulmonata: Agriolimacidae). American Malacalogical Bulletin 23: 137–156.


The American Malacalogical Bulletin provides a pdf file to authors without restriction on distribution.

The editorial office saw fit to make a large number of alterations to my submitted MS. Whilst some of these were improvements, they also introduced a substantial number of errors or unintended changes of meaning. I managed to get many of these reversed at the proofs stage, but, despite my begging entreaties, the editor refused to modify some phrases that substantially altered my original meaning (e.g. "very variable" had been replaced by "quite variable"). Accordingly, the pdf that I make available below has some corrections marked using Adobe's text-editing tool.
